Published: 13 Jun,
2023 CET.Updated: Fri 18 Aug 2023 09:59 CET

Sweden's terror threat level was raised in August from a level 3 ('increased threat') to a level four ('high threat'). This has left people both in Sweden and abroad asking whether it is a safe country. How does Sweden compare to other similar countries?

how do you define safety

    Safety can refer to many things, from the presence of international or domestic conflicts, to political instability, to the number of police officers in a certain country. The Institute for Economics and Peace, a global think tank dedicated to developing metrics to analyse peace and to quantify its economic benefits, is behind the Global Peace Index which ranks 163 countries yearly based on their levels of peace.
